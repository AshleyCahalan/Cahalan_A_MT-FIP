Brand Introduction

Introducing... Resolve Beer

Introducing a delicious and refreshing pale lager derived from one of the most popular beer recipes in Poland. This stunning beer is the result of the unlikely union with one of the most trusted cleaning brands in the industry. Top notch scientists have collaborated to produce this innovative beer recipe which will not only wow the toughest critic but also impress the staunchest neat freak. Coined "The Pet Lover's Beer", Resolve's full-body and satisfying taste is coupled with a revolutionary and 100% natural ability to remove pet stains. By simply pouring a sip's worth of beer on your carpet, you can avoid the nuisance and hassle of scrubbing and let Resolve do the work. This scientic marvel safely lifts stains from carpet fibres and dries odour-free. This fantastic beer will tantalize your taste buds and allow you to enjoy more quality time with friends, including of course, your furry friend!
